Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 6, 2025

1. Information We Collect

QuickC ("we," "our," or "us") collects the following information:

  • Location Data: Your current location to find nearby rides and calculate fares
  • Contact Information: Phone number for booking confirmation and driver communication
  • Ride History: Details of your bookings including pickup/drop locations, fares, and driver information
  • Device Information: Basic device data for app functionality and security

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Connect you with available ride providers in your area
  • Process and confirm your ride bookings
  • Provide customer support and resolve issues
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send booking confirmations and important updates

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only when necessary:

  • With Ride Providers: Your contact and location information is shared with selected drivers for ride completion
  • For Safety: Emergency contacts may be provided to authorities if required for safety reasons
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in our system
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Ride History: Kept for 3 years for customer support and legal requirements
  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Location Data: Only stored temporarily during active ride requests

7. Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with third-party services:

  • Ride Providers: Uber, Ola, Rapido, and other cab services have their own privacy policies
  • Payment Processors: Financial transactions are handled by secure payment gateways
  • Maps Services: Location services may use Google Maps or similar providers

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
